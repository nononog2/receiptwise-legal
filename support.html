Common issues

  • Export doesn’t work: CSV export is available for Pro users. Make sure you’re subscribed and try again.
  • Scan limit reached: Free users have a monthly limit. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited scans.
  • Wrong amount/date/category: You can edit any expense from the expense details screen.
  • Restore purchases: Open Settings → Subscription → Restore purchases.

Subscription & billing

  • Subscriptions are managed by Apple via your App Store account.
  • To manage or cancel: iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • If Pro isn’t recognized, try “Restore purchases” and reopen the app.

Data & privacy

  • Your expenses are stored on your device (unless you add cloud sync later).
  • We may use receipt text to extract fields (merchant, date, total, category).
  • Read the full details in the Privacy Policy.

What to include in your email

  • Device model and iOS version
  • ReceiptWise app version
  • What you expected vs what happened
  • (Optional) A screenshot — please avoid sensitive info if possible
