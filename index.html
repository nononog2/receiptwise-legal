Features

A focused workflow: scan → review → save → track. Built with modern iOS UI, clean spacing, and fast interactions.

Receipt scanning + review

Scan a receipt, then quickly verify extracted fields before saving.

Monthly totals

See “This month” spending at a glance, with totals grouped by currency.

Category breakdown

Understand where money goes with category insights and trends.

Multi-currency

Track expenses in different currencies without mixing totals.

Flexible monthly basis

Choose whether the month is calculated by scan date or receipt date.

Clean CSV export (Pro)

Export filtered expenses to CSV for spreadsheets or accountants.

CSV Export (Pro)

Choose a date range (This month / Last 30 days / All time), preview how many expenses will be exported, then share the CSV via iOS share sheet.

What’s included

Merchant, date, total, currency, category, tax (if present), notes (if present), and receipt text (if enabled).

Made for spreadsheets

CSV uses consistent numeric formatting and escapes values properly for clean imports.

Privacy

ReceiptWise is designed with privacy in mind. Your expenses are stored on-device by default. You choose when to export or share files.

On-device by default

Expenses and receipt images are kept locally unless you explicitly share exports.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Does ReceiptWise require an account?
No. You can use the app without creating an account.
How do subscriptions work?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. You can manage or cancel in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
What does Pro unlock?
Pro unlocks CSV export and other premium features as available.
